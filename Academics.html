Academic Programs at Lachoo Memorial College

Lachoo Memorial College provides a comprehensive selection of academic programs designed to promote academic growth and innovation in science, technology, business, and arts. Our courses span undergraduate, postgraduate, and research programs, designed to prepare students for the professional world with hands-on experience and in-depth knowledge.

Undergraduate Programs

Our undergraduate courses help students develop strong foundational skills in various streams. Popular programs include:

  • B.Sc. - Physics, Chemistry, Zoology, Botany, Biotechnology, and more.
  • B.Com. - Finance, Accounting, and Management.
  • BBA - Business Administration, Marketing, Entrepreneurship.
  • BCA - Computer Applications and Programming.

Postgraduate Programs

For students looking to specialize further in their field, we offer advanced postgraduate programs:

  • M.Sc. - Chemistry, Biotechnology, Physics, Mathematics.
  • M.Com. - Advanced Commerce and Finance.
  • MCA - Master in Computer Applications.
  • MBA - Management, Marketing, Human Resources.

Research & Ph.D. Programs

Our doctoral programs are driven by research excellence in various disciplines. Candidates work closely with experienced faculty on projects that push the boundaries of knowledge in:

  • Life Sciences
  • Physical Sciences
  • Business and Management
  • Computer Science
